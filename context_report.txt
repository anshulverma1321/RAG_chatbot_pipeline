Image Segmentation
Image Segmentation is a computer vision technique where an image is divided into multiple
regions, and each pixel is assigned a label representing a specific object or class.
Why Image Segmentation is Important
In robotics (especially your 4-wheel autonomous robot):
• Helps in environment understanding
• Enables path planning
• Improves obstacle avoidance
• Supports autonomous navigation
Example:
• Road → safe path
• Wall → obstacle
• Human → avoid
